How to Get Started

Welcome to Pharmacy Management! Follow these simple steps to begin using our platform:

  1. Create an account as a Customer or Vendor by signing up.
  2. Login to your account using your credentials.
  3. Explore pharmacies in your area or manage your pharmacy's inventory.
  4. Enjoy seamless management of medicines and prescriptions!
